Disableable E-Tanks

This option controls the ability to disable E-Tanks during gameplay. When enabled, move the cursor up into the E-Tank area of the HUD, select an E-Tank and press A to toggle it.

Disabling an E-Tank will also disable the tanks "above" it, and enabling an E-Tank enables all the tanks "below" it.

  • Off: Disables this feature: Energy Tanks cannot be disabled once collected.
  • Standard: Only empty E-Tanks can be disabled. To disable a full tank, Samus will have to take damage first in order to empty the tank.
  • Unrestricted: Any E-Tank can be disabled. Disabling full tanks will vent the excess energy, leaving Samus with 99 energy plus the remaining enabled tanks.
